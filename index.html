I'm interested in the intersections between disciplines, because rarely are the biggest problems in the world limited to a single subject area. I want to work to combine my many passions--whether in engineering, education, health, the environment, politics, or otherwise--into one.

Currently: working on the implementation of Opportunity Through Data, a project that plans to bring data science education to women's prisons. Our project recently received funding through the Big Ideas competition, placing in the top two in our category.
